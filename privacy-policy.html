1. Introduction

Welcome to Votch ("we," "us," "our," or "Company"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address and password (securely hashed)
  • Username or display name
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Bio or profile description (optional)

2.2 Watchlist & Activity Data

We collect information about your viewing preferences:

  • Titles you add to your watchlist
  • Watch progress (current episode, completion status)
  • Ratings and reviews you provide
  • Genres and preferences you select

2.3 Poll & Social Data

When you participate in polls and social features:

  • Polls you create and their content
  • Your votes on polls (including guest votes)
  • Friend connections and social interactions
  • Comments and messages (if applicable)

2.4 Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical data:

  • Device type, operating system, and version
  • Device identifiers and advertising IDs
  • App version and crash logs
  • IP address and general location (country/region)
  • Timestamps of your activities

2.5 Third-Party Data

When you import watchlists from other platforms (MyAnimeList, MyDramaList, Letterboxd, Trakt, Kitsu), you authorize us to access and process your data from those services.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: Creating and maintaining your account, authentication, and account recovery
  • Service Delivery: Providing Votch features, including watchlist management and poll functionality
  • Social Features: Enabling friend connections, polls, and shared experiences
  • Content Integration: Matching your watchlist data to titles from TMDB
  • Safety & Security: Detecting abuse and enforcing our Terms of Service

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Data Sharing

We share information with the following third-party services to deliver our application:

4.2 Supabase (Backend & Database)

  • We store all user data securely in Supabase's PostgreSQL database
  • Supabase provides authentication, real-time features, and cloud storage
  • Data is encrypted in transit and Row-Level Security (RLS) policies restrict unauthorized access
  • Supabase Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

4.3 TMDB (The Movie Database API)

  • We use TMDB API to fetch movie and drama metadata and images
  • Your search queries are sent to TMDB to match local titles to their database
  • TMDB images are never downloaded—we link directly from their CDN
  • TMDB terms apply to their usage: https://www.themoviedb.org/settings/privacy

4.4 No Sale of Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and sensitive data at rest
  • Authentication: Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms
  • Row-Level Security: Database RLS policies ensure users can only access their own data
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel can access user data for support purposes
  • Regular Audits: We monitor security logs and update our infrastructure
Note: While we implement best practices, no security system is completely impenetrable. We encourage you to use strong passwords to protect your account.

6. Your Rights

6.1 Data Access & Portability

You have the right to request a copy of your personal data in a portable format. Use the export feature in the app or contact us.

6.2 Correction & Updates

You can update or correct your account information at any time through your profile settings.

6.3 Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data. Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone. Some data may be retained for legal compliance.

6.4 GDPR & CCPA Compliance

If you are in the EU, UK, or California, you have additional rights under GDPR and CCPA. Contact us to exercise these rights.

7. Account Deletion

7.1 How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your account and all associated data, please contact us at: support@votchapp.com

Include "Account Deletion Request" in your email subject line, and we will process your request within 30 days.

7.2 What Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, we remove:

  • Email address and login credentials
  • Watchlist and viewing history
  • Ratings and progress tracking
  • Poll votes and preferences
  • Profile information and biography
  • Profile picture and personal settings
  • All user-generated metadata

7.3 What May Be Retained

For legal compliance and anonymization purposes, we may retain:

  • Anonymized poll data and vote counts (your individual votes are deleted)
  • Logs and usage records (stripped of personal identifiers)
  • Legal or audit records required by law

7.4 Permanent Action

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once deleted, your account and all associated data cannot be recovered.

7. Cookies & Tracking

7.1 Mobile App

Our mobile app does not use traditional cookies. Instead, we use:

  • Authentication Tokens: Securely stored to keep you logged in
  • Local Storage: For app preferences and offline functionality
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage data to improve performance

7.2 Web Presence

If you access our web resources, we may use cookies for authentication and analytics.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification. Your continued use of Votch after such updates constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
